Collect and organize information about your neighborhood with mobile or paper-based surveys.

LocalData is a toolkit for groups to gather place-based information about communities. With multiple ways to gather information, people of all ages, technical ability or access can efficiently collect important neighborhood-level information.

Write & Scan: Everyone can participate.

Write with pen and paper to answer custom survey questions in-the-field. To process your data, simply scan and upload documents to the website, and view your data immediately.

Mobile App

Collect information on buildings, streets and places using a smartphone in-the-field.

Coders & community groups: we need your help!

We're working with community organizations & the City of Detroit to build out this app. We'll be testing it out Summer 2012.Community groups: How can we make this tool best serve your needs? Let us know how & why you're interested, and we'll do our best to help you out.

Developers: We'd love your help! Our code is on GitHub and our stories are in Pivotal Tracker. The mobile app is built on HTML5, Javascript, and Leaflet. Server-side data processing is built on NodeJS, Flask, and PostGIS.
