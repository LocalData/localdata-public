Collect and organize information about your neighborhood with mobile or paper-based surveys.

LocalData is a toolkit that helps community groups gather neighborhood-level information. With multiple ways to collect this data, people of all ages, technical ability or access can efficiently manage and collect important neighborhood-level information.

Write & Scan: Everyone can participate

Write with pen and paper to answer custom survey questions in-the-field. To process your data, simply scan and upload documents to the website, and view your data immediately.

Mobile App

Survey with a smartphone (Androids and iPhones) and a data plan to collect data in the field. A simple interface makes it easy to for volunteers to submit accurate information quickly.

Roadmap

Community groups & coders: we need your help!

We're working with community organizations & the City of Detroit to build out this app. We'll be testing it out Summer 2012.

Community groups: How can we make this tool best serve your needs? Let us know how & why you're interested, and we'll do our best to help you out.

Developers: We'd love your help! Our code is on GitHub and our stories are in Pivotal Tracker. The mobile app is built on HTML5, Javascript, and Leaflet. Server-side data processing is built on NodeJS, Flask, and PostGIS. We're always looking for ways to make the project leaner, faster, and more robust.
